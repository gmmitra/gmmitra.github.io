Case Study

AvailAccess

My role was to create a simple user interface that has a streamlined flow of information and must be accessible for the visually impaired, physically disabled, and the elderly passengers. I conducted discovery sessions with the VP of product development, product owner, design team, and dev team to document business objectives and get a better understanding of the customer's pain points. Part of my work is to do research to identify key user attributes to focus on. I facilitated a design thinking ideation session to come up with new ideas for the booking feature.

Key Tools and DeliverablesAdobe XD, Photoshop, Illustrator, MTurk, Wireframes, Prototypes, Userjourney maps, User persona, User flow diagrams, Mockups

ResultNew design and layout of the app that has been handed to the development team that was built and deployed.

Case Study

Emirates Airlines VIP Food Catering

The project requirements was to create an online food catering ecommerce site for Emirates Airlines. The app will allow users to order food online for private flights and VIP flights. The orders can be manage through the admin dashboard. It also generates reports of the transactions.

My role is to to create the user journey for customers and admin users. I worked on the information architecture and created the userflow and wireframes showing the step by step sequence on how the users will interact with the website.

Key Tools and DeliverablesAdobe Illustrator, Flowchart, Wireframes, Userflow

ResultUser journey and wireframes for the online ordering and admin dashboard that was handed out to the web designers to design high fidelity mockups.
